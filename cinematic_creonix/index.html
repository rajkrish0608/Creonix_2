The Kit System

⚡

Paper Circuit Kit

Electricity demystified through paper, copper tape, and LEDs. Zero sharp edges, zero exposed components.

🧸

Play Max Kit

Snap-fit components in a tactile system that makes robotic logic feel like play. Every connection teaches reasoning.

🤖

Straw Bot Kit

Structural engineering through straw-based robotic assemblies. Design, iterate, and test real assemblies.

🕶️

AR / VR Kit

Headsets and AR cards rendering 3D models of circuits, anatomy, and cosmology in immersive space.

🏎️

Robotics ACR Kit

Unleash the speed! Build and program racing platforms, exploring motors, stability, and high-velocity controls.

👣

Human Following Robot

Teach robots to shadow you. Explores ultrasonic sensors, IR arrays, and real-time tracking algorithm implementations.

🖨️

3D Printers

Brings digital ideas to physical reality. Students design 3D models and print their own customized robotics brackets.

🔌

Arduino Kit

The gateway to microcontrollers. Learn breadboarding, basic C/C++ syntax, sensor inputs, and device control.

⚙️

Robotics Kit

A comprehensive mechanical platform featuring high-torque motors, structural metal brackets, and wireless controllers.

How It Works

01

School Signs MoU

Official partnership agreement. Transparent terms, student-first, and school-first structure.

02

Lab Setup

Creonix team designs, renovates, and installs modern benches, displays, and hardware inside the school.

03

Kit Allocation

Age-appropriate kits are supplied and catalogued based on the class grade curriculum.

04

Faculty Stationed

Certified Creonix educators begin teaching inside the school premises for the entire academic term.

05

Monthly Progress Reports

Detailed analytics of student outcomes are sent to school administrators and parents every month.
